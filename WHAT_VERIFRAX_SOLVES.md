# What Verifrax Solves

Verifrax produces a verifiable proof that a transformation happened deterministically and was frozen into an immutable record.

Inputs:
- Artifact (file)
- Verifrax baseline (tagged, frozen repo state)

Outputs:
- SHA-256 hash
- Certificate (verifrax.certificate.v1)
- Proof bundle (verifrax.proof.v1)
